Basic Information

Total Population

Dedtalai is a large village located in Khaknar of Burhanpur district, Madhya Pradesh with total 1000 families residing. The Dedtalai village has population of 4579 of which 2299 are males while 2280 are females as per Population Census 2011.

How To Reach

Asirgarh Road Rail Way Station , Chandni Rail Way Station are the very nearby railway stations to Dedtalai. How ever Bhusaval Jn Rail Way Station is major railway station 70 KM near to Dedtalai.By road way one can reach here by Khandwa-Amrawti road and Burhanpur-Amrawti road.
